Curriculum Vitae

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Institute of Information and Communication Technologies, Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, where I am supervised by Prof. D.Sc. Stoyan Mihov. My research focuses on leveraging automata theory to develop more expressive and computationally efficient language models by overcoming the fixed left-to-right generation order of traditional autoregressive models.

Research Interests

  • Formal Languages and Automata Theory
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Machine Learning
